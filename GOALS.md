# GOALS.md

## MVP outcome
An operator can import a timetable for one day or week, review sessions, generate Meet links, and publish/schedule the corresponding Google Classroom materials without manually creating every post.

## Success criteria
- reduce manual posting time sharply
- avoid duplicate posts
- show clear publish status per session
- support repeated weekly use

## Non-goals
- replace Google Classroom
- manage attendance or grading
- build student-facing experience
